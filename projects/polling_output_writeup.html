What Does Biden's Lead Mean?

Comparing Biden's Lead to Historical Victors.

Joe's History

With the 2020 election coming to a close, much has been said regarding Joe Biden’s polling lead over President Donald Trump. While different pollsters have disagreed over the strength of Biden’s polling lead, voters themselves are skeptical of polling after President Trump’s surprise victory in 2016. So how does Biden’s lead stack up?

Using FiveThirtyEight’s proprietary polling average - which attempts to reflect the current state of the polling in each state by calibrating various settings in the polling averages based on how well the polling average predicts future polls - we constructed a side-by-side comparison of each election-winner’s polling lead relative to the number of days before the election.
